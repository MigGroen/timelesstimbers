About Us

TIMELESS TIMBERS

Your professional carpenters that specialize in Kitchens, Bathrooms, Bedrooms, Carpentry and Custom Made Furniture. Timeless Timbers is an established company in Stellenbosch that will not compromise on excellence. Our team takes pride in our quality workmanship. We strive to leave a lasting impression for our services and craftsmanship. Please feel free to browse through our website that will provide you with a glance of the creative designs we have installed. Whether you are renovating or building, Timeless Timbers can design and install the product of your dreams using A-grade materials. Making your experience a pleasant one.

Our Services

Kitchen Cabinets and remodel

Timeless Timbers, we specialize in crafting custom kitchen cabinets and providing expert carpentry for kitchen remodels that transform your space into the heart of your home. Our skilled craftsmen use premium materials and innovative designs to create cabinets that are both functional and beautiful, tailored to your style and needs. Whether you're updating a modern kitchen or enhancing a traditional one, our attention to detail and commitment to quality ensure a flawless finish that elevates your kitchen's aesthetic and value. Let us help you build the kitchen of your dreams.

Bath Cabinets and remodel

At Timeless Timbers, we offer custom bath cabinets and remodel carpentry designed to enhance both the style and functionality of your bathroom. Our expert craftsmen create beautiful, durable cabinetry tailored to your space, using top-quality materials to ensure long-lasting results. Whether you're seeking a sleek, modern look or a more traditional design, we bring your vision to life with precision and care. Trust us to deliver a stunning bathroom transformation that combines elegance with practicality.
